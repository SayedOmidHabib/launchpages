FAQ

Questions, answered

Is our data used to train AI models?
No. Your content is indexed for retrieval only, encrypted at rest, and never used to train shared or third-party models. You can delete the index at any time.
How does Nova handle permissions?
Nova mirrors your source permissions in real time. If someone can't open a doc in Notion, they'll never see its content in an answer — not even a summary.
What sources does Nova support?
Slack, Notion, Google Drive, Confluence, Linear, Jira, Zendesk, Intercom, GitHub and 15+ more. Anything else is reachable over our API and webhook ingest.
Can we self-host?
Enterprise plans offer dedicated single-tenant deployment in your cloud region of choice, with custom retention windows and a signed DPA.

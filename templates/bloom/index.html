Why Bloom works

Kind to your brain, ruthless with results

Built with behavioral scientists so the streak serves you, not the other way around.

Grow a real garden

Each habit is a plant. Consistency makes it flower; rare species unlock at 30, 90, and 365 days.

Grace days, not guilt

Two grace days a month keep your garden alive, missing once shouldn't erase a month of work.

Nudges that read the room

Smart reminders learn your rhythm and go quiet on weekends, holidays, and rough weeks.

Grow together

Share a greenhouse with friends. Cheer streaks, plant duo-habits, water each other's gardens.

Insights that teach

Bloom finds your patterns, like "you run 3× more after 8 hours of sleep", and helps you use them.

Private by design

Your data stays on your device unless you say otherwise. No ads, no selling, ever.
